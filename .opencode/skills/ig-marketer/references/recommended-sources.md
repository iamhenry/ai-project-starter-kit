# Recommended Sources

Start research here. These are high-signal sources for organic social growth in the app/wellness space. Use `web_search` to find relevant articles, then `web_fetch` to read them.

After checking these, expand to general web search for niche-specific content (sobriety, wellness, habit tracking).

## Content Strategy (what to post, how to think about virality)

- **Social Growth Engineers** — socialgrowthengineers.com
  Organic social marketing strategies for mobile apps. Daily updates on TikTok/Reels growth tactics. Search: `site:socialgrowthengineers.com [topic]`

- **Superwall Blog** — superwall.com/blog
  Podcast recaps and deep dives on app growth. Focus on distribution and organic content articles, skip paywall/conversion optimization (not relevant yet). Search: `site:superwall.com/blog [topic]`

## App Growth & Monetization (how downloads become revenue)

- **RevenueCat Blog** — revenuecat.com/blog

- **Sub Club Podcast** — subclub.com
  By RevenueCat. Interviews with founders of top subscription apps. Blog recaps available. Search: `site:subclub.com [topic]`

## Research Rules

- **Recency:** Filter all queries to last 3 months. Older content may reference dead trends.
- **Depth:** Read 3-5 articles per cycle, not 20. Extract patterns, not volume.
- **Signal over noise:** Skip paywall A/B testing, price optimization, onboarding flows — those are post-download problems. Focus on: what content formats get views, what hooks work, what CTAs drive downloads.
