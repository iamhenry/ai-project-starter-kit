# Keyword Direction Guide

Use this guide as an optional source of focused starting directions for Phase 1 discovery when the user gives no seed or a broad category seed.

This is a vocabulary and exploration aid, not market evidence. Category membership, need-lane tags, and multi-need annotations do not prove demand, payment, entry opportunity, or tiny-bet fit. Do not require coverage across categories, score candidates by category or need level, or reject a candidate because it falls outside this guide. The list is non-exhaustive and can be extended when App Store evidence suggests a better direction.

Each category and subcategory is a broad theme to expand, not a required search term or canonical idea. Generate several related user jobs, pain points, synonyms, and app-level search hypotheses around a selected direction. The listed examples are illustrative starting points, not a fixed keyword list, complete market coverage, or a limit on the ideas to explore.

## Need Lens (Above Categories)

Maslow sits one level above these themes. It answers where to look, not whether demand exists.

Ladder: need level → felt problem → app job → concrete App Store phrase → Astro.

| Need | Look for jobs about… |
| --- | --- |
| Physiological | sleep, food, hydration, basic body care |
| Safety | health, money security, privacy, stability |
| Love / belonging | relationships, dating anxiety, family, connection |
| Esteem | status, appearance, confidence, recognition, skill proof |
| Self-actualization | mastery, purpose, creative expression, peak habits |

Do not search abstract labels (`esteem`, `belonging`). Convert first: Esteem + height anxiety → `height predictor`. Multi-need is an annotation only (e.g. Esteem + Belonging), never a strength score.

For no-seed or broad-category runs, pick one need lane to enrich seed variety (add a second only if the batch is weak or clustered). Merge resulting phrases into the existing discovery pools; do not create a need-lane pool or quota.

## How To Use It

1. Select a few directions that fit the discovery context; do not use every category in one run.
2. Optionally start from a need lane, then pick tagged directions under it — or pick directions first and note their tags.
3. Expand each selected direction into several related user jobs and concrete App Store search phrases describing those jobs or problems.
4. Keep the phrases as hypotheses until Astro validates popularity, difficulty, intent, and result shape.
5. Keep the existing profile-aligned, adjacent, and wildcard discovery pools active.
6. If the focused starting batch is weak, broaden into adjacent directions and the existing pools rather than stopping or rejecting the category.

Prefer phrases that describe an app-level job over broad market labels or startup concepts. For example, a focus direction might produce `focus timer`, `distraction blocker`, or `deep work planner`; these are illustrative hypotheses, not canonical terms to search literally or use exclusively. Prefer evidence-derived phrases after Astro exposes relevant competitors.

## Productivity

_Needs: Safety, Esteem, Self-actualization_

- Role-specific tools for teachers, sales reps, writers, clinicians, or real estate agents
- Focus, deep work, distraction management, and digital wellness
- AI note-taking, summarization, second-brain, and personal knowledge management
- Voice transcription and organization for meetings, lectures, or notes
- Offline-first and privacy-focused productivity utilities

## Personal Finance Micro-Tools

_Needs: Safety, Esteem_

- Budgeting for irregular, freelance, contractor, or gig income
- Subscription cancellation and management
- Credit card optimization and rewards tracking
- Debt payoff visualizers and calculators
- Retirement, Coast FIRE, and financial independence planning

## Health And Fitness

_Needs: Physiological, Safety, Esteem_

- Senior, low-impact, balance, mobility, and fall-prevention fitness
- Prenatal and postpartum fitness
- Sport-specific training for running, climbing, BJJ, swimming, or similar activities
- Recovery, sleep, hydration, and wearable-informed tracking
- Habit and streak tracking for focused behaviors or chronic-condition routines

## Photo, Video, And Creative Utility

_Needs: Esteem, Self-actualization, Love / belonging_

- Image-to-video tools for short-form social content
- AI photo editing, styles, transformations, and trend-based effects
- Background removal, asset generation, and product-creative utilities
- Podcast and short-form clip creation and editing
- Thumbnail testing and caption optimization

## Education And Skill Coaching

_Needs: Esteem, Safety, Self-actualization_

- Niche exam preparation and spaced repetition
- AI tutors or coaches for specific skills
- Professional upskilling, career transitions, and job readiness
- Gamified or micro-learning for targeted outcomes
- Parent- or teacher-facing learning progress and analytics

## AI Agentic Apps

_Needs: Safety, Esteem_

- Personal task automation for email, scheduling, research, or forms
- Vertical customer support and issue-resolution workflows
- Shopping, replenishment, and gift-finding assistance
- Onboarding, HR, and repetitive workflow automation
- Content and marketing campaign planning or optimization

## Female Health

_Needs: Physiological, Safety, Love / belonging_

- Menopause and perimenopause tracking and support
- Fertility, cycle, and ovulation tracking
- Pregnancy and postpartum support
- Pelvic health and condition-specific gynecological tracking
- Hormone monitoring and personalized wellness

Health, finance, and other sensitive directions remain subject to the existing risk, trust, privacy, and feasibility gates.
